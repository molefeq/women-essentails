About us

In today’s fast pace of life, time is a luxury a lot of us women do not have. From been a business women, to a mom to a wife and course between all of this the need to look and feel beautiful. Have you ever needed to do your hair and nails the same day but not been able to use your current Salon as they are fully booked or decided you need some me time and no Salon you know can assist you in that short space of time? Or you just want find a Salon with a good special, saving money is key! Women’s essentials is the first unique platform that allows you the facility to find all your beauty requirements in one place This online app links you to all the Salons in your area, where you can review their services and pricing without moving an inch. You can view this info on your way to work, during your lunch break, while making dinner, in bed before you close your eyes or three hours before you need to host a dinner/function that was dropped on you 10 minutes ago!

How does it work

Choose what service you require (nails, hair, waxing, etc.) and women’s essentials we take you to all the Salons offering that service in a 5km radius of you? Choose a Salon. Info we appear giving you information and a pricing indication from that Salon. If you are happy with the Salon click the icon take me there.
